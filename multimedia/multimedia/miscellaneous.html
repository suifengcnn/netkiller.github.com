6.1.1.1. To convert .rm files to .mp3

Command line utility mencoder can convert .rm files to .mp3

mencoder input_file.rm -ovc frameno -oac mp3lame -of rawaudio -lameopts cbr:br=128 -o output_file.mp3

6.1.1.2. wav to mp3

lame -h -b 128 audiodump.wav myconvertedfile.mp3

6.1.1.3. encode to Macromedia Flash format

mencoder input.avi -o output.flv -of lavf \
    -oac mp3lame -lameopts abr:br=56 -srate 22050 -ovc lavc \
    -lavcopts vcodec=flv:vbitrate=500:mbd=2:mv0:trell:v4mv:cbp:last_pred=3

6.1.1.4. rm to wav

$ mplayer -cache 10 -ao pcm -aofile stream.wav  rtsp://XXXX/stream.rm
$ lame stream.wav

$ mplayer test.rm -ao pcm:file=audiofile.wav -vc dummy -vo null
$ lame -h -b 128 audiofile.wav audiofile.mp3
$ rm audiofile.wav
